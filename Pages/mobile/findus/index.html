GETTING HERE

Getting to Montana Mountain has never been easier. 
BY CAR

The park is a 50-minute drive from Helena, Montana and can be reached from Highway 302. 

BY TRAIN

There’s an Amtrak stop just 500 meters from the main park entrance, with regular daily service.

BY BUS

Several high-end coach companies offer day trips and overnight trips to Montana Mountain.
