Our Mission Statement

We maintain and improve the value of our homes in Shore Acres. We accomplish this by community projects such as the attractive entryways and signs you see when you enter Shore Acres, working to get a new recreation center, saving the fire station from moving, improving parks, keeping the Code department engaged to fix neglected properties, and we work with the Police to keep our neighborhood SAFE. Through this, and many other projects, we maintain and improve our quality of life in this island community.
